# The Wind Waker — Heart Pieces checklist

All **44** [Pieces of Heart](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Piece_of_Heart) in [*The Legend of Zelda: The Wind Waker*](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/The_Legend_of_Zelda:_The_Wind_Waker). With the three hearts Link starts with and six from bosses, the maximum is **20 hearts**.

Source: [Zelda Dungeon Wiki — The Wind Waker Heart Pieces](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/The_Wind_Waker_Heart_Pieces) (converted to a personal checklist; verify in-game if anything differs on your version).

---

## Checklist

- [ ] **#1 — Forsaken Fortress** — Bottom floor of the room with the map. Step on the switch behind the barrels to open the jail cell; chest inside.
- [ ] **#2 — Windfall Island** — Win [Hide-and-Seek](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Hide-and-Seek) with the [Killer Bees](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Killer_Bees); [Ivan](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Ivan) rewards the piece.
- [ ] **#3 — Windfall Island** — Beat Salvatore’s [Sinking Ships](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Sinking_Ships) in under 24 shots. *Needs ~10 Rupees to play.*
- [ ] **#4 — Pawprint Isle** — Enter the center dome, drop into the grotto, defeat [Red ChuChus](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Red_ChuChu) along the path; chest at the end.
- [ ] **#5 — Dragon Roost Island** — Complete the 25-letter [Mail Sorting](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Mail_Sorting) for [Baito](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Baito), mail his letter to [Kashiko](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Kashiko) (postbox outside near the [King of Red Lions](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/King_of_Red_Lions)); reply letter contains the piece.
- [ ] **#6 — Mail** — After [Forbidden Woods](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Forbidden_Woods) and [Farore's Pearl](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Farore%27s_Pearl), read the [Rito Chieftain](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Rito_Chieftain) letter from a postbox.
- [ ] **#7 — Five-Star Isles** — Salvage sunken treasure with [Treasure Chart](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/The_Wind_Waker_Treasure_Charts) **#33**.
- [ ] **#8 — Five-Star Isles** — Submarine south of the isles: defeat enemies, open chest.
- [ ] **#9 — Angular Isles** — Salvage treasure using the chart for this location ([Treasure Chart](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/The_Wind_Waker_Treasure_Charts) **#15**).
- [ ] **#10 — Angular Isles** — On the larger island, push/pull blocks to the top; chest there.
- [ ] **#11 — Headstone Island** — [Hyoi Pear](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Hyoi_Pear): control a [Seagull](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Seagull), grab the piece on the island peak, return to [Link](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Link_(The_Wind_Waker)).
- [ ] **#12 — Diamond Steppe Island** — Salvage sunken chest with Treasure Chart **#23**.
- [ ] **#13 — Needle Rock Isle** — Hyoi Pear: fly seagull to the top, hit the switch, return to Link; flames around chest disappear.
- [ ] **#14 — Greatfish Isle** — [Deku Leaf](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Deku_Leaf): climb the small island peak, set wind northwest, float to the nearby ledge chest.
- [ ] **#15 — Six-Eye Reef** — Submarine southwest: defeat three [Moblins](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Moblin), climb [ladder](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Ladder), open chest.
- [ ] **#16 — Orca** — Sword training: hit Orca **500** times in his challenge.
- [ ] **#17 — Stone Watcher Island** — [Cannon](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Cannon): destroy cannons on the enemy outlook platform nearby, climb up, open the chest.
- [ ] **#18 — North of Tingle Island** — Cannon or [Boomerang](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Boomerang): defeat [Big Octo](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Big_Octo), [Grappling Hook](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Grappling_Hook) the treasure.
- [ ] **#19 — Spectacle Island** — Salvatore’s [Barrel Shoot](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Barrel_Shoot) cannon game. *Needs ~50 Rupees.*
- [ ] **#20 — Windfall Island** — Win the nightly [Auction House](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Auction_House) bid (bring lots of Rupees).
- [ ] **#21 — Windfall Island** — [Deluxe Picto Box](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Pictograph_Box): color photo of [Linda](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Linda) for [Anton](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Anton_(The_Wind_Waker)); later speak with Linda at the café after they’re dating.
- [ ] **#22 — Crescent Moon Island** — Salvage with Treasure Chart **#11**.
- [ ] **#23 — Seven-Star Isles** — Cannon or Boomerang: defeat the 12-eyed Big Octo, collect treasure.
- [ ] **#24 — Dragon Roost Island** — Give the guard outside the [Rito Chieftain](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Rito_Chieftain) **20** [Golden Feathers](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Golden_Feather); check postbox for girlfriend’s letter.
- [ ] **#25 — Flight Control Platform** — [Bird-Man Contest](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Bird-Man_Contest): Deku Leaf across the goal (cyclones, upgraded magic meter help).
- [ ] **#26 — Bomb Island** — Bomb the rock in the island center; clear the grotto (Magtails, switches), chest at the end.
- [ ] **#27 — Forest Haven** — Salvage with Treasure Chart **#31**.
- [ ] **#28 — Three-Eye Reef** — Salvage with Treasure Chart **#38**.
- [ ] **#29 — Star Island** — [Secret Grotto](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Secret_Grotto) under a rock: defeat enemies, open chest.
- [ ] **#30 — Windfall Island** — Plant **14** plants from [Zunari's Shop](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Zunari%27s_Shop) around the island; talk to the man on the bench when all pots are filled.
- [ ] **#31 — Rock Spire Isle** — Buy from [Beedle's Shop Ship](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Beedle%27s_Shop_Ship) for **950** Rupees.
- [ ] **#32 — Rock Spire Isle** — Cannon: destroy two ships near the isle, salvage treasures (one is the piece).
- [ ] **#33 — Southern Fairy Island** — Salvage with Treasure Chart **#4**.
- [ ] **#34 — Pawprint Isle** — Salvage with Treasure Chart **#30**.
- [ ] **#35 — Traveling Merchants** — Complete the [Trading Sequence](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/The_Wind_Waker_Trading_Sequence); trade the [Shop Guru Statue](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Shop_Guru_Statue).
- [ ] **#36 — Withered Trees (various)** — Water the last [Withered Tree](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Withered_Tree) with [Forest Water](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Forest_Water); piece pops out.
- [ ] **#37 — Windfall Island** — [Fire Arrows](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Fire_Arrow): behind Salvatore’s game, ladder + switch for windmill; re-enter game, climb left stairs outside, jump windmill at peak, Fire Arrow into spinning center; speak to [Kreeb](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Kreeb).
- [ ] **#38 — Windfall Island** — After lighting the windmill: Deku Leaf to the small island chest (**night only**).
- [ ] **#39 — Windfall Island** — [Maggie](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Maggie_(The_Wind_Waker)) on [Auction House](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Auction_House) top floor: mail [Maggie's Letter](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Maggie%27s_Letter) to [Moe](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Moe), cutscene, café [Ilari](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Ilari) for [Moblin's Letter](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Moblin%27s_Letter), give to Maggie.
- [ ] **#40 — Rock Spire Isle** — Salvage with Treasure Chart **#2**.
- [ ] **#41 — Outset Island** — [All-Purpose Bait](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/All-Purpose_Bait): carry the giant black pig across the bridge, bait the dirt patch left of the path so it digs up the piece.
- [ ] **#42 — Bomb Island** — Salvage with Treasure Chart **#20**.
- [ ] **#43 — Thorned Fairy Island** — Salvage with Treasure Chart **#5**.
- [ ] **#44 — Two-Eye Reef** — Salvage with Treasure Chart **#46**. *GameCube: also available after all 50 [Savage Labyrinth](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Savage_Labyrinth) floors on [Outset Island](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Outset_Island).*
