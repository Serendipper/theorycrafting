# The Wind Waker — Heart Pieces checklist

All **44** [Pieces of Heart](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Piece_of_Heart) in [*The Legend of Zelda: The Wind Waker*](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/The_Legend_of_Zelda:_The_Wind_Waker). With the three hearts Link starts with and six from bosses, the maximum is **20 hearts**.

Source: [Zelda Dungeon Wiki — The Wind Waker Heart Pieces](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/The_Wind_Waker_Heart_Pieces) (converted to a personal checklist; verify in-game if anything differs on your version).

Progress is saved in **this browser only** (local storage on GitHub Pages). It does not sync across devices; clearing site data resets the checklist.

## Checklist

Grouped by island (heart piece **#** matches the [wiki numbering](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/The_Wind_Waker_Heart_Pieces)).

### Outset Island

- [ ] **#16** — Sword training with [Orca](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Orca_(The_Wind_Waker)): hit him **500** times.
- [ ] **#41** — [All-Purpose Bait](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/All-Purpose_Bait): carry the giant black pig across the bridge; bait the dirt patch on the left so it digs up the piece.

### Windfall Island

- [ ] **#2** — Win [Hide-and-Seek](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Hide-and-Seek) with the [Killer Bees](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Killer_Bees); [Ivan](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Ivan) rewards the piece.
- [ ] **#3** — Beat Salvatore’s [Sinking Ships](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Sinking_Ships) in under 24 shots. *~10 Rupees to play.*
- [ ] **#20** — Win the nightly [Auction House](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Auction_House) bid (bring lots of Rupees).
- [ ] **#21** — [Deluxe Picto Box](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Pictograph_Box): color photo of [Linda](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Linda) for [Anton](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Anton_(The_Wind_Waker)); later speak with Linda at the café after they’re dating.
- [ ] **#30** — Plant **14** plants from [Zunari's Shop](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Zunari%27s_Shop) around the island; talk to the man on the bench when all pots are filled.
- [ ] **#37** — [Fire Arrows](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Fire_Arrow): behind Salvatore’s game, ladder + switch for the windmill; re-enter, climb left stairs outside, jump the windmill at peak, Fire Arrow into the spinning center; speak to [Kreeb](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Kreeb).
- [ ] **#38** — After lighting the windmill: [Deku Leaf](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Deku_Leaf) to the small island chest (**night only**).
- [ ] **#39** — [Maggie](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Maggie_(The_Wind_Waker)) on the [Auction House](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Auction_House) top floor: mail [Maggie's Letter](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Maggie%27s_Letter) to [Moe](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Moe), cutscene, café [Ilari](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Ilari) for [Moblin's Letter](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Moblin%27s_Letter), give to Maggie.

### Dragon Roost Island

- [ ] **#5** — [Mail Sorting](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Mail_Sorting): complete the 25-letter sort for [Baito](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Baito), mail his letter to [Kashiko](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Kashiko) (postbox outside near the [King of Red Lions](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/King_of_Red_Lions)); reply letter contains the piece.
- [ ] **#24** — Give the guard outside the [Rito Chieftain](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Rito_Chieftain) **20** [Golden Feathers](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Golden_Feather); check the postbox for his girlfriend’s letter.

### Forest Haven

- [ ] **#27** — Salvage sunken treasure with [Treasure Chart](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/The_Wind_Waker_Treasure_Charts) **#31**.

### Forsaken Fortress

- [ ] **#1** — Bottom floor of the room with the map: step on the switch behind the barrels to open the jail cell; chest inside.

### Angular Isles

- [ ] **#9** — Salvage with Treasure Chart **#15**.
- [ ] **#10** — Larger island: push/pull blocks to the top; chest there.

### Bomb Island

- [ ] **#26** — Bomb the rock in the island center; clear the grotto (Magtails, switches), chest at the end.
- [ ] **#42** — Salvage with Treasure Chart **#20**.

### Crescent Moon Island

- [ ] **#22** — Salvage with Treasure Chart **#11**.

### Diamond Steppe Island

- [ ] **#12** — Salvage with Treasure Chart **#23**.

### Five-Star Isles

- [ ] **#7** — Salvage with Treasure Chart **#33**.
- [ ] **#8** — Submarine south of the isles: defeat enemies, open chest.

### Flight Control Platform

- [ ] **#25** — [Bird-Man Contest](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Bird-Man_Contest): [Deku Leaf](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Deku_Leaf) across the goal (use cyclones; upgraded magic meter helps).

### Greatfish Isle

- [ ] **#14** — [Deku Leaf](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Deku_Leaf): climb the small island peak, set wind northwest, float to the nearby ledge chest.

### Headstone Island

- [ ] **#11** — [Hyoi Pear](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Hyoi_Pear): control a [Seagull](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Seagull), grab the piece on the peak, return to [Link](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Link_(The_Wind_Waker)).

### Needle Rock Isle

- [ ] **#13** — Hyoi Pear: fly seagull to the top, hit the switch, return to Link; flames around chest disappear.

### Pawprint Isle

- [ ] **#4** — Center dome → grotto: defeat [Red ChuChus](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Red_ChuChu) along the path; chest at the end.
- [ ] **#34** — Salvage with Treasure Chart **#30**.

### Rock Spire Isle

- [ ] **#31** — Buy from [Beedle's Shop Ship](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Beedle%27s_Shop_Ship) for **950** Rupees.
- [ ] **#32** — [Cannon](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Cannon): destroy two ships near the isle, salvage treasures (one is the piece).
- [ ] **#40** — Salvage with Treasure Chart **#2**.

### Six-Eye Reef

- [ ] **#15** — Submarine southwest: defeat three [Moblins](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Moblin), climb [ladder](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Ladder), open chest.

### Seven-Star Isles

- [ ] **#23** — [Cannon](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Cannon) or [Boomerang](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Boomerang): defeat the 12-eyed [Big Octo](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Big_Octo), collect treasure.

### Southern Fairy Island

- [ ] **#33** — Salvage with Treasure Chart **#4**.

### Spectacle Island

- [ ] **#19** — Salvatore’s [Barrel Shoot](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Barrel_Shoot) cannon game. *~50 Rupees.*

### Star Island

- [ ] **#29** — [Secret Grotto](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Secret_Grotto) under a rock: defeat enemies, open chest.

### Stone Watcher Island

- [ ] **#17** — [Cannon](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Cannon): destroy cannons on the enemy outlook platform nearby, climb up, open chest.

### Thorned Fairy Island

- [ ] **#43** — Salvage with Treasure Chart **#5**.

### Three-Eye Reef

- [ ] **#28** — Salvage with Treasure Chart **#38**.

### Two-Eye Reef

- [ ] **#44** — Salvage with Treasure Chart **#46**. *GameCube: also from all 50 [Savage Labyrinth](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Savage_Labyrinth) floors on Outset Island.*

### Great Sea (open water)

- [ ] **#18** — North of [Tingle Island](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Tingle_Island): [Cannon](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Cannon) or [Boomerang](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Boomerang) vs [Big Octo](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Big_Octo), [Grappling Hook](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Grappling_Hook) the treasure.

### Quests (multiple locations)

- [ ] **#6** — After [Forbidden Woods](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Forbidden_Woods) and [Farore's Pearl](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Farore%27s_Pearl), read the [Rito Chieftain](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Rito_Chieftain) letter at any postbox.
- [ ] **#35** — [Trading Sequence](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/The_Wind_Waker_Trading_Sequence): trade the [Shop Guru Statue](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Shop_Guru_Statue) to a [Traveling Merchant](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Traveling_Merchant).
- [ ] **#36** — Water the last [Withered Tree](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Withered_Tree) with [Forest Water](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/wiki/Forest_Water) (any of the eight trees on the Great Sea).
